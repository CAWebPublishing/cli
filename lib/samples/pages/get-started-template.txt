<h2>A Template Site for Getting Started</h2>
[/et_pb_text][et_pb_text admin_label="Intro Text" _builder_version="3.0.106" background_layout="light"]

This site is yours to edit and create as you like. You mainly utilize the content modules of the "Divi builder" to create and design your web pages. The Divi builder enables major flexibility and modular design reuse, allowing you to create custom page templates for every type of page you may publish regularly. The pre-built pages in this site are examples you can use as a starting point, for ideas, or to be simply deleted when you have no more use for them.

The following is a list of short "How to's" to do common tasks:

A "News Page" is nothing more than a page with a summary list of News Articles embedded on it.

In CAWeb, News Articles are created from the Admin page using the "Posts" content type. While editing a post, your first must select the "Use the Divi Builder" button to activate the Divi Builder editor. Then you select the "Posts Detail" module to publish your news article.
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><em>Note: if you are not already familiar with using the Divi Builder, open the item above titled "A great introduction to the Divi Builder."</em></p>
Then to display a summary list of your news article posts on a page, you create a page, and within the Divi Builder you use a "Posts List" module to display the summarized list of news articles.

You can find an example of this by viewing the underlying content published on the example "<a href="/post-lists/news-list/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Press Releases</a>" page. Simply edit this page, or use it as an example of how to start building your own. You can find the Posts that make up the summary on this page by navigating to the "Posts" section of your Admin page of your site.

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-179" src="posts-admin.png" alt="" width="490" height="311" />
